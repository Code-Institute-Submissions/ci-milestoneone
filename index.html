The Monkees

We formed in Los Angeles in 1965 specifically for the American television series 'The Monkees', which aired from 1966-1968. The quartet is made up of Americans Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith and Peter Tork with Englishman Davy Jones. The telesvision show was cancelled in 1968 but we continued to record music until 1971. We habe sold more than 75 million records worldwide.
